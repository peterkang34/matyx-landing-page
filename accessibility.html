Accessibility Statement

Matyx Agency LLC · Last updated July 21, 2026

Our commitment

Matyx Agency LLC is committed to making matyx.com accessible to individuals with disabilities. We aim to meet or exceed the W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and we treat accessibility as ongoing work, not a checkbox.

What we do

  • Semantic HTML structure, with proper headings and landmarks, so screen readers can navigate the site.
  • Descriptive alternative text on meaningful images.
  • Color contrast designed to meet WCAG AA standards.
  • Full keyboard navigability, with visible focus on interactive elements.
  • Support for the reduced motion preference — animations are disabled for visitors who have it turned on.
  • Text that scales with browser zoom and system font-size settings.
  • Ongoing review of the site as content and features change.

Third-party content

Some content on our site comes from third parties — such as embedded YouTube videos. We cannot always control the accessibility of third-party content, but we choose providers that offer accessible players and captioning where available.

Feedback

If you have difficulty accessing any part of this website, or have ideas that would make it more accessible, we genuinely want to hear from you. Please contact us at sales@matyx.com or (405) 212-4178. If applicable, include the web address (URL) and a description of the problem you encountered — we will do our best to fix it promptly.
